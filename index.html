The Crystal Collector Game!

Ready to Play? Here's how it works:


  1. You will have to guess the number of crystals. You will be given a random number of crystals at the beginning of the game: "Number To Conquer"

  2. There are four crystals below.

    • Blue Crystal
    • Green Crystal
    • Red Crystal
    • Yellow Crystal

  3. when you click on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

  7. The game restarts whenever you win or lose. Another opportunity to conquer the game!
